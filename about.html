SCHOOL HISTORY OF MARY MOTHER OF MERCY PRE & PRIMARY SCHOOL

Mary Mother of Mercy Pre & primary school is an undertaking of the Mary Mother of Mercy Catholic Parish Mji Mpya Relini under the registered trustees of the Arch Diocese of Dar es Salaam.

Founded in 2011: The school started at the level of the Pre-Primary administered by the Sisters of the Holy Spirit.

Expanded in 2014-2015: Developed to primary school level with a new campus constructed within the church premises.

Modern Facilities

Both Pre-primary and Primary School campuses feature modern complex structures with contemporary classrooms, administration offices, resting and first aid rooms. The Primary School is equipped with a computer lab, library and a conference room.

Official Registration: Operating under Ministry of Education, Science and Technology with registration numbers DS.1/7/EA1.127 and D.1/7/127
