ABOUT US

MARY MOTHER OF MERCY PRE & PRIMARY SCHOOL

The school started at the level of the Pre-Primary in 2011 administered by the Sisters of the Holy Spirit.

The school was conducive to carry out the Pre-Primary school activities most effectively, admitting children from 3 years to 5 years in the three levels – Baby, Pre-primary I, Pre-primary 2 classes.

The school developed to a primary school level in the year 2014 and in 2015 a new school campus for primary school pupils was constructed within the church premises.

Both Pre-primary and Primary School campuses have modern and malt complex structures with modern class rooms, decent administration offices, resting and first aid rooms.

The Primary School is equipped with a computer lab, library and a conference room.

Our Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive curriculum from Grade I to VII, following the Tanzanian National Education Standards. Our programs are designed to build strong foundations in core subjects while nurturing creativity and critical thinking skills.

Grade I & II Foundation

Age Group: 6-8 Years

Building essential skills in Reading, Writing, Listening & Speaking, Arithmetic, Kiswahili, Culture & Arts, and Health & Environment. Our foundation program focuses on developing basic literacy and numeracy skills.

Grade III & IV Intermediate

Age Group: 9-11 Years

Advancing to English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Science, Geography & Environment, Historia ya Tanzania & Maadili, and Culture & Sports. Students develop stronger academic foundations and critical thinking.

Grade V-VII Advanced

Age Group: 12-14 Years

Comprehensive studies in English, Kiswahili, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies, and Civic & Moral Education. Preparing students for secondary education with advanced academic skills and moral values.

Extracurricular Activities

Focus: Holistic Development

Beyond academics, we offer Sports, Arts, Music, Drama, and Cultural Activities. Our programs develop leadership skills, teamwork, creativity, and physical fitness for well-rounded education.
